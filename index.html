About Us

Who are we

We started as a team Elite Evolvers on 19th April 2023, with a clear vision: to unite all Pokémon Unite players from West Bengal under one banner. What begun as a WhatsApp community, we quickly expanded our reach and impact. By March 2024, we launched our monthly Bengal Cup tournaments and established our Discord server. Recognizing the importance of connection, we organized regular meetups, capturing these moments on our Instagram. To share our competitive spirit and learning opportunities, we launched our YouTube channel for tournament streaming. Our journey has led us to connect and play with national-level players, expanding our reach beyond West Bengal. Notably, our Monsoon Cup, the first-ever Pokémon Unite themed tournament in India, marked a significant milestone, opening our community to all players across the nation. Join us and be a part of our evolving story!
